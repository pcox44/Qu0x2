🎲 Daily Dice Game

Use each of the five dice exactly once, and any operations (+, −, ×, ÷, parentheses) as needed, to get as close as possible to the target number. The closer you get, the better your score — a perfect match gives you a score of 0. You can play multiple times per day, but only perfect scores count toward your streak. Dice must be separated by operations; you can't combine digits like 4 and 5 to make 45.
